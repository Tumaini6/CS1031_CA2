Visit Aberdeen

Trading

Aberdeen has always been known for being a centre for trade. Fishing has played the largest role in its history and still is very important today. There has been many other trading industries through Aberdeen such as:

  • Oil
  • Granite
  • Textiles
  • Paper

Granite

Granite has also played a massive role in Aberdeen’s history. This can be very clearly seen through it’s architecture. The granite quarried from Rubislaw Quarry wasn’t just used in Aberdeen it’s self, the granite went into building a sizable portion of the Houses of Parliament and Waterloo Bridge in London. The quarry has since been filled in with water and used as a tourist attraction.
